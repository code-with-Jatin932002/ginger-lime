About Us

Welcome to Ginger & Lime

Ginger & Lime brings together the dishes our guests crave most: aromatic Thai curries, delicate rolls and salads, and bold Chinese plates—all made with care in our kitchen.

Whether you are planning a family dinner, a date night, or a quick lunch, we focus on fresh ingredients, balanced flavours, and hospitality that makes you want to come back.

More about Ginger & Lime

We are glad you want to know us better.

Our menu is built around three kitchens in spirit—Thai, Japanese, and Chinese—so you can explore green and red curries, sushi and salads, noodles, dim sum, momos, and sizzlers without leaving your table.

Every dish is prepared when you order, using vegetables and sauces we balance for heat, sweetness, and aroma. Ask our team for pairing tips or spice levels—we want your meal to feel personal.
